About School

Welcome to Adward School, a nurturing haven nestled in the heart of Rajshahi. With a team of dedicated educators and a vibrant community of 120 little angels, our school is a beacon of learning and growth. At Adward, we believe in fostering not just academic excellence, but also holistic development, nurturing young minds to reach their fullest potential. With a warm and welcoming environment, coupled with a curriculum designed to inspire curiosity and creativity, we strive to empower each child to thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Join us at Adward School, where every child's journey is cherished and celebrated.

Our Teachers

Our esteemed team of teachers at Adward School embodies a diverse tapestry of talent, passion, and dedication. From Mrs. Sonali Das's innovative teaching methods to Mr. Mohammad Ahmed's unwavering commitment to student success, our educators strive to create an environment where every child feels valued and empowered to thrive. Vehicles Facility

At Adward School, we understand the importance of safe and reliable transportation for our students. That's why we offer a comprehensive vehicle facility to ensure hassle-free commuting for our students. Our fleet of well-maintained buses and vans are equipped with modern amenities and supervised by trained drivers and assistants, prioritizing the safety and comfort of every child. Whether it's picking up students from designated stops or dropping them off at their doorstep, our transportation service is designed to provide convenience and peace of mind to parents and students alike. With regular maintenance checks and adherence to safety protocols, parents can trust that their children are in good hands while traveling to and from school. At Adward School, we strive to make every aspect of the educational journey smooth and secure, including transportation.
